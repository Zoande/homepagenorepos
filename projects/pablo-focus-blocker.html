Overview

Focus Blocker is a privacy-focused Microsoft Edge extension that helps you regain control of your browsing experience. Using Edge's efficient Declarative Net Request API, it blocks distracting websites with zero lag, no data collection, and no compromise on your privacy.

🎯 Why Focus Blocker? In a world of infinite distractions, maintaining focus is the ultimate competitive advantage. Focus Blocker makes it effortless.

Privacy First

🔒 No Tracking

We never collect, store, or analyze your browsing data. Your privacy is protected by design.

⚡ Zero Latency

Uses Edge's Declarative Net Request for instant blocking with minimal performance impact.

🛡️ Local Only

All settings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

Powerful Features

📋 Customizable Rules

Create specific blocklists for different contexts: work mode, study mode, or pure relaxation mode.

🎨 Preset Categories

Pre-configured blocklists for social media, news sites, gaming, and other distraction categories. One click to enable.

🔄 Smart Redirects

Intelligently redirect blocked sites to productive alternatives. Transform distraction into motivation.

⏰ Schedule Blocking

Set time-based blocking rules. Auto-enable focus mode during work hours, automatically disable during breaks.

📊 No Analytics

No telemetry, no tracking, no analytics. We literally cannot see what you're blocking or when.

☁️ Cross-Device Sync

Sync your blocklists across Windows, Mac, and Linux. Consistent focus everywhere you work.
